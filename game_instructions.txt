+STANDARD:
The main game mode of Dynamic Noughts and
Crosses AI! Play against an AI on a grid
which grows when you win and the win length
decreases when you draw. The game ends when
you lose once. The objective is to get to as
big of a grid as you can. Your score will
increase as you win - compare your high score
with other players on the leaderboard.

+IMPOSSIBLE 3X3:
Play against an unbeatable AI on a 3x3 grid!

+PLAYER VS PLAYER:
Play against a friend with a customisable
grid size and win length!
